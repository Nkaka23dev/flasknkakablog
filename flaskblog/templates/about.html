About The Blog

This blog was created for sharing opinions on different topics . It doesn't give you ability to comment or like other people's posts I am looking forward to allow people to comment and like posts, but it allows you to create posts and delete them.

This blogpost allow you to create account and manage it the way you want. it gives you a full access to your account. You can update your account as well as your profile picture, for other people to recognize you, if you do not upload a photo a default photo will be assigned to you.

You can check the number of posts you have, just click on your username. For updating post and deleting post you need to click on post title after being authenticated and you will be redirected to the page where you can delete and update post.?

In case you forget your password or if it is known by other people, you need to request a password reset token which will be sent to your email account. if you provide a wrong email account you won't be able to reset your password.
